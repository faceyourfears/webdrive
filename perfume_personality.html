About Perfume x Personality

Perfume x Personality is all about personalizing the right perfume for your character and needs. This project has been in development for the last 5 years and we're ecstatic to pick the best and befitting fragrance for you.

How does it work?

You can pick 2 personality traits from the given column and press generate to find which "Dior" fragrance meets your desire.
